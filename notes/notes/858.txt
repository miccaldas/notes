In Vim, this is the commands to comment
and uncomment a series of lines.
----------------------------------------
UNCOMMENT:
- Put cursor in first '#',
- Press 'Ctrl-v',
- Go down to the last commented line,
- Press 'x',
- Press 'Esc'.
----------------------------------------
COMMENT:
- Go to first line you want to comment,
- Press 'Ctrl-v',
- Select until last line to comment,
- Press 'Shift-i',
- Press '#',
- Press 'Esc'.
